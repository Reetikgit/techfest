PLAY-TRIX


General rules:-
The team should consist of a maximum of two members.
Participants will not be allowed to use mobile phones or any other electronic gadgets inside the hall.
Any change in the team members should be notified in prior.
Necessities like paper will be provided.
Every round is an elimination round .
The decision of the judge will be final and are not subjected to any further changes.

Description:-

Picsword (visual):-
Rapid fire round.
The participants should guess the word looking at the picture clues given.
Negative points for wrong answers.

Tech charades :-

One of the participants should act and the other participant should guess the word.
Time limit is 3 minutes per word.
Giving clues for letters of the word is strictly not allowed.

Greedy Knight(board game) :-

A chess board with each box consisting of one coin and knight is given to a team.
The team should collect the maximum number of coins where the knight should be moved as per the chess board rules.
The knight cannot be moved to a box without a coin
The time limit for the game will be 2 minutes.
Final round (surprise):- The winners will be announced based on the score they obtain. The rules of the game will be revealed onspot.
