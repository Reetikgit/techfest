Tekno-watt

GENERAL RULES
PARTICIPANTS EXCEEDING THE TIME LIMIT WILL BE DISQUALIFIED
USE OF ANY ELECTRONICS OR PHONES ARE PROHIBITED
WINNERS ARE DECIDED ACCORDINGLY TO THE TIME UTILIZATION AND THE SOLUTION PROVIDED BY THE TEAM FOR A PROBLEM

ROUND 1 -GUESS WHO I AM?

PARTICIPANTS HAVE TO IDENTIFY THE COMPONENTS DISPLAYED

ROUND 2-SOLVE IT AND RIG UP

PARTICIPANTS ARE GIVEN RIDDLES AND SHOULD SOLVE THEM AND IDENTIFY THE COMPONENTS IN THE GIVEN TIME .
RIG UP THE CIRCUIT USING THE COMPONENTS AND SHOW THE OUTPUT

ROUND 3-CROSSWORD
PARTICIPANTS ARE GIVEN A CROSSWORD PUZZLE AND SHOULD SOLVE IT IN THE GIVEN TIME
SELECTION CRITERIA WILL BE BASED ON THE NUMBER OF CORRECT ANSWERS OBTAINED BY THE TEAM

ROUND 4- MYSTERY ROUND

RULES ARE UNREVEALED FOR NOW
BONUS ROUND
THIS ROUND IS USED TO SELECT THE TEAM WHO PROGRES IN THE GAME IF THE COMPETITION IS HIGH
